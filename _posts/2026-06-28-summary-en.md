## [Google Restricts Meta's Gemini Access Amidst AI Compute Shortage](https://www.ft.com/content/c5d52f72-71ef-40bc-bad3-61afdba8b378) ⭐️ 8.0/10

Google has limited Meta's access to its Gemini AI models because Meta's requested compute capacity exceeded Google's available supply, a restriction that has been in place since March and has delayed some of Meta's internal AI projects. This situation highlights a significant bottleneck in the AI industry's compute power, impacting major tech companies and underscoring the intense competition and scarcity of resources required for advanced AI development. Meta is encouraging more efficient AI token usage and accelerating its development of in-house models, while Google has reportedly secured a significant compute capacity lease from SpaceX to expand its own resources.

**Background**: AI models like Gemini process information in units called tokens, which are fundamental to their training and inference capabilities. The demand for compute power to train and run these large models has surged, leading to a global shortage of specialized hardware like GPUs.
